Students

For prospective students: I am looking to recruit MSc/PhD students for Fall'22. Topics of interest include sequential decision-making (reinforcement learning/bandit problems) and optimization for machine learning. The plan is to work at the intersection of theory and practice - developing scalable, robust algorithms with theoretical guarantees on their performance.

If you are interested in working with me, please apply to SFU CS and mention my name in your application. The admissions committee will then consult me iff your credentials meet the department requirements.

Typically, emailing me beforehand does not improve your chances of admission. If you do email me, please (i) include your CV and (ii) go through my publications, pick a paper on RL/Bandits or Optimization, and summarize it, describing its merits/demerits and possible extensions. Doing a good job on this will definitely improve your chances, though there are no guarantees and you will still need to meet all the department requirements.
If you have emailed me, I have likely seen your application. Unfortunately, I am unable to respond to every email, and will only reach out to selected students.
